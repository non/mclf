Other Hands

Other Hands is a VR experience for people curious about the relationship between their meat bodies and virtual bodies.

Other Hands uses Oculus Rift 1.1 DevKit, Leap Motion, and ink, with special guest Adrienne Kammerer.

Other Hands inscribes meaning on our physical bodies in response to our decisions in virtual worlds.
